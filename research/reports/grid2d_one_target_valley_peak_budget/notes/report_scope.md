# Scope

This note is intentionally narrower than `grid2d_one_target_exit_timing`.

It keeps three ideas only:
- the shared one-target geometry
- a representative double-peak panel with a clearer pre-collapse valley case
- direct valley-vs-peak2 timing/time-budget comparisons

It does not try to repeat the full continuation scan, the full critical-band narrative, or the complete early/late/no-exit decomposition.

The new right-hand metrics are:
- exact outside-time share inside each window-conditioned occupancy budget
- a membrane-specific post-first-crossing remaining-time proxy

The first quantity answers “how much of the window-conditioned pre-hit time is actually spent outside the corridor”.
The second answers “once a genuine membrane crossing happens, how much of the total hit time budget still remains afterwards”.
